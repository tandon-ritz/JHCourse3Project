### FEATURE DESCRIPTION


* Subject 

	-An identifier of the subject who carried out the experiment.
	-There are 30 subjects.  Each observation displays the Id of the subject that has carried out the experiment.

* Activity 

	-Activity Label
	
		1 WALKING

		2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS

		3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS

		4 SITTING

		5 STANDING

		6 LAYING
